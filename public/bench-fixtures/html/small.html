Quick tips

  • JPEG — Best for photos. Lossy compression; quality below 70% may introduce artefacts.
  • WebP — Best overall compression. Supported in all modern browsers.
  • PNG — Lossless. Use for screenshots, icons, or images with transparency.
  • Max dimension — Downscales the longer side before encoding. Useful for reducing large photos.
